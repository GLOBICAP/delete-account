Delete Your Account

Last updated: October 28, 2025

You can request deletion of your Globiy account and associated personal data by following the steps below. After your request is processed, your account will be permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the Globiy app and ensure you are signed in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Account.
  3. Select Delete Account and confirm.

If you cannot access the app, you can also email us from your account email address:

  • Send an email to support@globiy.app with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  • Include your full name and any relevant account details to help us locate your account.

What Happens Next

  • We will verify your request and identity.
  • Once verified, we will permanently delete your account and associated personal data, except where retention is required by law.
  • We will notify you by email once the deletion is complete.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or data retention, contact us at support@globiy.app.
